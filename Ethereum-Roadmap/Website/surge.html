Proto-Danksharding

×

Summary

Proto-Danksharding introduces a new transaction type that carries large data "blobs," which are cheap to include on the consensus layer but are not processed by Ethereum's execution layer. This approach limits the computational burden on nodes, as they only store these blobs for a limited time. Proto-Danksharding lays the groundwork for future scalability upgrades, particularly full Danksharding, by setting up the necessary cryptographic and technical infrastructure.

Danksharding

×

Ethereum's sharding roadmap has evolved from an idealistic, to a pragmatic, rollup centric approach. As shown below in the Ideation chart, the Ethereum community original pursued sharding at the execution layer (Full Sharding). Gradually, and in conjunction with the rollup centric roadmap, leading research began to favor sharding only on the data, or consensus, layer (Data Sharding). Again, the community shifted in favor of Danksharding , which is not technically sharding at all, but rather a probabilistic data sampling approach requiring the introduction of second transaction type, blobs. Proto-Danksharding is the first of two iterations of this approach.

Ideation

  • Full Sharding
  • Data Sharding
  • Danksharding
  • Proto-Danksharding

Proto-Danksharding is slated for inclusion in the upcoming hard fork, Cancun-Deneb, as specified by EIP-4844. It is likely that Danksharding will follow in a subsequent upgrade. Beyond these two, it remains to be seen if additional upgrades will be required to further scale Ethereum. In the medium term, it is reasonable to consider these two sufficient.
