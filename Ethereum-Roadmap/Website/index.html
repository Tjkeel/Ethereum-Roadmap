Ethereum Roadmap

Ethereum has a rough roadmap, as published by Vitalik. However, he posts roadmap updates very rarely, and even if he did post them more frequently, the community shouldn't rely upon a single individual to declare Ethereum's optimal path forward, or even its current status on the agreed upon targets.

The following aspires to by a dynamic, crowd sourced interpretation of the community’s desired roadmap.
